Choosing between renting and buying

Why rent laptops?

  • No large upfront cost, pay monthly instead
  • No surprise repair or upgrade bills
  • Quickly scale up or down as your team changes
  • Always have access to the latest laptops and technology
  • Maintenance and repairs are included
  • Ideal for temporary staff, projects, events, or periods of rapid growth

Why buy laptops?

  • One-time purchase, you fully own the laptops
  • No ongoing rental payments
  • Keep the devices for as long as you want
  • You handle all repairs, updates, and replacements
  • Higher risk of outdated laptops over time
  • Bigger investment needed if your business grows or needs change
