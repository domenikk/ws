Donating IT

Need to replace or get rid of excess IT equipment? Then donate the residual value of your old company hardware to a charity of your choice.

The ITdonations Foundation is an initiative of Circular IT group and makes donating easy, transparent and secure.

100% secure data deletion

Security is crucial for ITdonations when disposing of your excess hardware. 100% secure data deletion is guaranteed. We use Blancco software, a certified method with a host of advantages:

  • GDPR compliant
  • Report and certificate issued
  • Approved by the Dutch General Intelligence and Security Service
  • Procedure to prevent data breaches
