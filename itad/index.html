What is ITAD?

IT Asset Disposition (ITAD) is the process of securely disposing of outdated or unwanted IT hardware, including laptops, computers, servers, switches, routers, and mobile devices. The goal is to recycle or refurbish items responsibly, while ensuring data security. Compliance with legal regulations is key.

As a leading ITAD company, Circular IT group ensures secure data wiping and environmentally friendly recycling. We also offer asset recovery, turning your old tech into value through buy-back schemes. Recover IT assets to safeguard data, meet ESG targets, and reduce total cost.

Circular IT group specialises in ITAD services, including surplus IT equipment disposal, data centre dismantling, certified data erasure, and computer recycling. We handle global disposals for medium to large organisations and are trusted by more than 90 multinationals. We provide clear, audit-ready reports and certificates for GDPR and sustainability targets.
